Bookstore is based in Toronto

We are three students at the university of Laurier who have a passion for reading and books. When Covid-19 hit, we were all stuck inside this was the time we would read our books.

However we found it hard to find any good book recommendations online with fellow book lovers, so we came up the idea to create a site where we dicuss our favoruite books and buy new ones with a click of the button!
